Linguistics survey: Rate possible meanings of sentences

You are being asked to take part in a research study being done by Hayley Ross, a graduate student at Harvard University, and Kathryn Davidson Zaremba, a professor at Harvard University. It is your choice whether or not to participate in this research. If you choose to participate, you may change your mind and leave the study at any time. If you leave the study, please return your submission on Prolific by selecting the 'Stop without completing' button.

If you choose to be in the study, you will complete a questionnaire. This questionnaire will help us learn more about meaning and English sentence structure. The questionnaire will take you around 6-10 minutes to complete. Please complete this questionnaire only if you are fluent in English and English is the first language you learned.

If you participate in this study, you will be given an English sentence and asked to rate how natural the sentence sounds. There are no foreseeable risks from participating in this study.

The data we collect from this survey is confidential. Please do not include your name or other information that could be used to identify you in the questionnaire responses.

Aggregate, de-identified data may be made available to researchers outside of the research team associated with this project.
